Do your homework

You'll need to know the business and the customer inside out. How do people currently view the product, what preconceptions are they disposed to, and finally: what are you trying to accomplish.

Build relationships

No matter how good your idea someone is going to not like it, three are going to want a different focus, two ambivalent and one is not going to want to pay for it. Negotiating your way through design by committee is a necessary skill for design-driven outputs.
