What does a designer do for my company?

Communicates. Information. Visually.

You have at most three seconds to make an impression. If the purpose of your design isn't immediately apparent you've lost the chance to connect with your target audience. The hard truth is that almost everyone who looks at your company is going to glance at it. You need to make the absolute most of those few seconds.

It's nearly impossible to meet this need with text alone

Properly designed visuals impart tons of information at a glance. The moment you look at something you've already retained an impression about

  • What is the purpose, or message being relayed
  • How does this relate to what I'm currently doing?
  • Do I recognize this as being part of something else?
  • Who is communicating this message
Not to mention next level intangibles like: 'How much effort was put into this'
